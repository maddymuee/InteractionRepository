images & methods

Meg Hunt

I've recently rediscovered Meg Hunt. She works with a lot of different tools, both traditional and digital. I think she's a great example of intentional texture use.

Darren Booth

Darren Booth is a great lettering artist who I found while I was researching for a hand-lettering assignment. I'm interested in hand-lettering but don't feel confident in my abilities!

Sarah Beth Morgan

I've been looking at the work of Sarah Beth Morgan, an illustrator, designer, and art director for animation and motion graphics. Her art is deceptively simple; she uses textures and colors really wonderfully and subtly. I'm finding myself drawn pretty often to art direction positions and the work of art directors.

Work has been in a rut this week. I am having trouble in Interaction in particular, pushing through the lack of defined expectations/making myself experiment. I'm sort of realizing I don't really have fun sketching/playing/experimenting without a defined goal and I'm wondering if that's something I can change about myself.
