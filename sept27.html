readings

We read Pedro Paramo in writing this week and concurrently I watched 2001: A Space Odyssey for the first time. Both super art. (The second thing is not strictly a reading, but bear with me.)

Partially because it's what we were explicitly talking about in writing, I thought a lot more about style and influence in relation to the book and the movie. Both of these works were extremely influential in their respective genres. The conclusion my professor came to in writing was that works of art are remembered because their styles are unique. THEN, he told us to "just be ourselves" when we make art. I feel like I'm being given conflicting advice this year: should I think about my "style" or not?
